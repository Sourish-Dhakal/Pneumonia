Pneumonia Treatment and Prevention

Treatment of Pneumonia

A short description

Viral Pneumonia has a lot of flu-like symptoms and can cure by itself. Bacterial Pneumonia usually needs a hospital visit and is more common. Antiviral medications can treat Viral Pneumonia, but it's not very needed because it can go away by itself usually. Antibiotics treat Bacterial Pneumonia and Antifungal medications treat fungal pneumonia. If you need more oxygen because of Pneumonia, they can hook you up and give you oxygen therapy. IV Fluids can be sent into your veins ot make sure you aren't dehydrated. (Because of the liquids and pus your lungs bleed because of the Pneumonia.) They can also drain the fluids/pus out of your lungs with surgery.

How to reduce the symptoms of Pneumonia:

  • Pain relievers and fever reducers like Advil or Tylonol can help with body aches and fever.
  • If you go to the doctor, there might be breathing treatments and exercises the doctor tells you to do to make sure you can breathe easier.
  • Get a humidifier, it can make it easier to breathe.
  • Drink water, water is good for you. During Pneumonia, you especially need more because you bleed fluids.
  • Be healthy, not only to reduce Pneumonia but in general because being healthy is cool.

Prevention of Pneumonia

Prevent Other Infections like COVID-19 and Flu

The cause of Pneumonia is the immune system attacking the lungs to stop an infection, so if you prevent infections that cause Pneumonia and you'll be preventing pneumonia.

Get Vaccines

You can get vaccines like Pneumovox23 or Prevnar13.

Stop Smoking

Smoking can cause Pneumonia.

Wash hands with soap and water

This prevents infections that cause Pneumonia.

Practice Phsyical Distancing if you or someone else has an infectious disease.

Don't get infected by anyone else.

Avoid lots of alcohol

It's unhealthy. Alcohol is bad for you and can weaken your body.
