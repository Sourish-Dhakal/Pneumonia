Symptoms

Symptoms of Pnrumonis

A short description

There are different symptoms if depending on if you have Viral or Bacterial pneumonia. Viral Pneumonia has a lot of flu-like symptoms and can cure by itself. Pneumonia is very similar to the common cold and the flu. Only a healthcare provider can diagnose you, so if you have any of these symptoms, you should go ask a healthcare provider:

  • Congestion / Chest Pain
  • Difficulty Breathing
  • Fever of 102 degrees fahrenheit, or 38.88 degrees
  • Celsius, or higher
  • Coughing up colored mucus

Bacterial and Viral Symptoms and Long Term Symptoms

Bacterial Pneumonia Symptoms

  • Fever to 102 degrees fahrenheit or 38.88
  • degrees celsius
  • Cough with colored mucus
  • Tiredness
  • Rapid Breathing
  • Shortness of Breath
  • Rapid Heart Rate
  • Sweating or Chills
  • Chest Pain / Abdominal pain
  • Loss of appetite
  • Bluish skin, lips or nails
  • Changes mental state

Viral Pneumonia Symptoms

  • Dry Cough
  • Headache
  • Muscle Pain
  • Extreme tiredness or weakness
  • Some bacterial Pneumonia symptoms

Long Term Symptoms

  • Decreased ability to exercise
  • Worsening of cardiovascular disease
  • Mental decline
  • Quality of life decline
  • More risk of chronic lung diseases (Children who recovered from pneumonia)

Children get different symptoms.
The symptoms for people in old age include:

  • A sudden change in mental state
  • Low appetite
  • Fatigue
